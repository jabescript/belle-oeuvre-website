About Us

Belle is a retail partnership between Belle Oeuvre and Belle Piante. Both established in 2019 by Matthew Gabriel Cotas. Belle Oeuvre is known for their customizable crafts and souvenirs made from wood and acrylic. Belle Piante, on the other hand, specializes in a variety of succulent plants.


Types of Products

Acrylic Case Cap Collection

A display enclosure for your collectibles (e.g. hats, toy cars, & shoes) made out of high acrylic, which provides excellent protection, with a unique and luxurious look. .

Acrylic Print Engraving with Lamp Base

A personalized LED decorative lamp, engraved with an image of your choice.

Wood Picture Engraving

Laser-engraved photos on wood with high quality details to uniquely and permanently archive and display your most cherished photos.

Laser Crafting Souvenir

Laser-cut and laser-engraved wooden products that can be used for decorations, souvenirs, or personalized gifts.

Cactus Rose with Wood Pot

Succulents

Wood and Acrylic Case Collection
